Inauguration Event

Date: June 3rd, 2024

Time: 09:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Location: Lecture Hall 2

The Department of Computer Science Engineering (AI-ML) at Dayananda Sagar University proudly hosted the inaugural event for the IEEE Robotics and Automation Society (RAS) and the Computational Intelligence Society (CIS) on 3rd June 2024. This landmark occasion highlighted the university's commitment to cutting-edge research and innovation in robotics, automation, and artificial intelligence. Esteemed faculty, industry experts, and enthusiastic students gathered for the event, which included an insightful technical talk by renowned speakers, Dr. Sumana Ashok and Dr. Mangala Gowri. The event celebrated collaboration, knowledge exchange, and the promise of shaping the future through transformative technologies.
